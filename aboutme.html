About me Who is Jing-An Chen? Jing-An is a Taiwanese designer who currently live in Ottawa, Canada. As a designer, Jing-An is not satisfied with merely drawing some beautiful sketches on paper, he loves creating something real, touchable, interactable. This idea drives him to grasp the Beachler's degree of Industrial Design and even become a professional Full-Stack Designer. In Taiwan, designers are not only designers. The working market in Taiwan always wants designers to be all-knowing. To satisfy his employer, Jing-An pushed himself to learn as much as possible. Finally, Jing-An becomes a light-speed learner, he can grasp any specialty in one week, and his skills has across so many different fields including: Technical Drawing Ergonomics UI & UX Data Analytics Marketing Video Editing Photography Animating 3D Modeling Project Management Psychology Patent Analytics Tele Sales Email Sales Search Engine Optimizing Woodworking Metalworking Electrical Engineering Bodybuilding Weightlifting To name but a few. Meanwhile, Jing-An is a movie and comedy lover. Inspired by Richard Sapper, a German industrial designer. Jing-An believes a good design is just like a good joke. It must be fun, joyful, able to twist audiences’ assumption, and triggering the audiences to enjoy the funny part once and once. This idea grows and becomes Jing-An's motto: “When it comes to design, humour matters” A designer who designs like a comedian; A photographer who shoots like a director; An engineer who works like an artist. Everything is different, everything is connected, like plants, like broccoli. This is Broccoli Design. This is Jing-An Chen.
